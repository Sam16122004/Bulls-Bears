Event details

Fintasia on the other hand, is a superhero theme based asset allocation game. It is based on asset management and distribution, teams will be given wide range of assets including bonds ,stocks ,crypto, fd, gold, real estate, etc. Each team will get one magical power which they can use in any round. Before every round teams will destroy other teams assets based on certain financial news and events hoping to destroy the other teams wealth . In the end team with highest amount of assets wins.
