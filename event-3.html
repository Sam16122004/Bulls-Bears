27 September 2023

Fin-Shooting : Two teams, one straight shot

DATE: 27 September 2023,Wednesday

TIME: 4 p.m. - 6 p.m.

VENUE: E-203

INTRODUCTION

Bulls&Bears, the finance club of PDEU, was delighted to host their first event of the current academic year. The event,“Fin-shooting” was held on Wednesday September 27th, 2023. The event aimed at collaborating finance with an exciting twist of games, creating an atmosphere of both education as well as enjoyment. It held special importance for the club as it was their first event with the subcommittee.

The event started with our President, Parva Jani, giving his warm regards to the audience and welcoming them. He then introduced himself and gave an insight of the club and its working. Following this, our Vice President, Umang Sukhadia and General Secretary, Soham Patel, took the flow of the event in their hands by extending regards, introducing themselves and giving an overview of the event, including a demonstration of how the game was to be played. The event was hosted by Yash Parekh, Content & Documentation head, along with Kush Shah, Public Relations head.

Event Overview and Activities

The event started at 4:15, with all the participants present. The hosts of the event gave a comprehensive briefing on the rules and regulations of the game so that the participants were clear about the game. There were total fifteen teams consisting of four members each which summed upto a total of sixty participants. Two teams had face off against each other where they were asked all finance related questions that they had to answer in 30 seconds. If they fail to do so, the opponent team gets an opportunity to shoot at any of the team members against them with a soft ball. If both of the teams failed to answer the question, then it was left to the audience to answer it. Whosoever answers the same correctly, gets promoted to the next level.

The first round was played by the General Secretary and the Vice President of the club for the demo where participants could get clarity about the game. After two rounds and witnessing some intense rounds of competition. Four teams were selected for the semifinals where they had to compete against each other for the final round. The four teams were:

1. Stocking stocks 2. Financial baazar 3. Faarzi 4. Scam

Out of these four teams “stalking stock” and “financial bazaar” were the finalists who competed against each other to win the game. After the game "stalking stock" was announced as the winner of the game. The winning team got a shoutout on our Instagram page along with a smiley ball.

Audience

15 teams registered themselves with four participants in each team. In total, there were sixty members in the event filled with enthusiasm. Most of the participants were from 1st year of SLS, B-tech and a lot of them were passionate about finance. The audience was very enthusiastic which added to the success of the event. They had great teamwork and sportsmanship while playing. It was an absolute pleasure for the club to host such an amazing audience.

Conclusions

“Fin-Shooting”, Bulls&Bears’s inaugural event was a great success, leaving lasting memories for all who attended. Participants, core members, and subcommittee actively took part in the games, fostering a sense of friendship and shared enjoyment throughout. The game helped everyone develop the spirit of teamwork and sportsmanship, along with making contacts with like minded people. With this, we eagerly anticipate all our future events filled with similarly enriching and enjoyable gatherings.
