Success History of Bulls and Bears

BullsandBears, the finance club of PDEU, was founded in year 2018, with an aim to improve financial literacy amongst students within and beyond the campus. The club tries to do so by updating their social media followers with the ongoing current affairs in the financial world. We also conduct various seminars, events and games to educate students about finance through fun. After that, there was no looking back, the club has expanded and flourished ever since.

Overview

The Finance Club at PDEU aims to bring together all students who share an interest and passion for finance and investments and share their knowledge and insights with each other. It aims at enabling them to discover recent trends and development in the field while allowing each individual to grow personally. We aim to provide the students with a hands-on experience by organizing developmental opportunities through guest speakers, panel discussions, mock stock market simulation and many more such events in the field of finance.

Mission & Goals

  • The purpose of the finance club is to promote interest and impart their knowledge I'm the field of finance.
  • To become a space of learning in the field of finance and investment that creates successful professionals.
  • Foster an environment among students to encourage continuous learning and discovery of recent trends in finance.
  • Help members develop practical investment skills and test them in the markets, and create superior access to career opportunities in the investment industry.
