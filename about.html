SUCCESS STORY OF BULLS AND BEARS

BullsandBears, the finance club of PDEU

BullsandBears, the finance club of PDEU, was founded in year 2016, with an aim to improve financial literacy amongst students within and beyond the campus. The club tries to do so by updating their social media followers with the ongoing current affairs in the financial world. We also conduct various seminars, events and games to educate students about finance through fun. After that, there was no looking back, the club has expanded and flourished ever since.
