Event details

Bazaar is a stock market simulation where participants will be provided with a mock number of stocks and cash to experience the thrill of stock market. The ultimate goal of Bazaar 5.0 is to maximize your portfolio's value. At the end of the event, the participant with the highest portfolio value will win and walk away with real cash prizes and vouchers!

The events will help the participants gain hands-on experience in stock market trading, develop critical financial analysis and decision-making skills, explore the intricacies of market dynamics and price movements as well as network with like-minded finance enthusiasts and compete for exciting cash prizes and vouchers.
